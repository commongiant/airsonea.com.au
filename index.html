How It Works

Use AirSonea anywhere to take an accurate measurement of your WheezeRATE™.
The AirSonea sensor detects and digitises breathing sounds, and via Bluetooth® these are transferred to your smartphone. The digitized sounds are then transmitted to the cloud and analysed for the presence of wheeze.
